Research

My research focuses on developing models for steering low-density regions of diffusion models. My work explores diffusion guidance for broad priors by leveraging the density of an external model as JEPA-SCORE. I enjoy building tools and models that expand the creative and scientific possibilities of computer vision and machine learning.
